Updates and Announcements:

November 10th, 2022

The stopdogehate.org Website is (mostly) Complete!

By the time the school year ended, only the basic functions of the website where completed. And once again, other personal commitments and the next year's school work put the project on hold. However, it was not forgotten.

Almost two years later, the stopdogehate.org website was released to the world.

April 5th, 2021

The stopdogehate.org Project is Born!

At this point, the idea had been on the Creator's mind for a while. However, limited free time due to school and other commitments meant the idea had to wait. As luck would have it, the Creator's digital arts teacher tasked the class with an open-ended creativity project, and the Creator knew exactly what to do for it.

On this day, the stopdogehate.org domain name was purchased, and work began on the new website.
